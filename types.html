Explore Our Top Coffee Types

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It's known for its strong and rich flavor.

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a coffee drink made with espresso and equal parts of steamed milk and milk foam. It's often topped with cocoa or cinnamon.

Cafe Latte

A cafe latte is made with espresso and steamed milk. It has a milder coffee flavor and is often served with a small amount of milk foam.

Americano

Americano is a diluted espresso made by adding hot water to it. It has a flavor closer to traditional drip coffee.
